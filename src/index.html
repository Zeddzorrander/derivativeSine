In this part, you will once again enter a restriction on the range. However, this time, we will enter the interval using a center and a radius, and our function will have some issues. We want to investigate what is happening at these problem points. There are three new sliders: Zoom, Left Endpt, and Right Endpt. The Zoom slider will allow you to zoom in and out on the point we are currently invesitigating. The Left and Right Endpoint sliders will move the respective endpoints of the restricted domain. Clicking on one of these endpoint sliders will allow you to move the endpoint of the interval in increments of 0.001 units.
